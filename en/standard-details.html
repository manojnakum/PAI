[KSS 1:2006] Automotive - Safety Glass - Methods of testing optical properties

General Details

Technical Committee No. TC02
Status Active
Date of Publication 18/02/2016
Sector Mechanical Sector
Sub Sector -
Standard Type Technical Standard

Field

This standard is concerned with optical test methods related to the safety requirements of the safety glass used in the vehicle, whatever its kind or other materials
